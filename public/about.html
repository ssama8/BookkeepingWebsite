About

In 1998 astay-at-home-Mom started a bookkeeping business. She began with one client and provided personal treatment and exceptional customer service. This caused her business to grow over the next several years solely by word of mouth. Ten years later she had more than twenty-five clients and not enough time in a day. Her husband got more involved in the business, employees were retained, and the business name was changed to TOMAndSON Bookkeeping. Now several years later, our mission is still the same, treat our clients as partners looking out for their best interests while providing a much needed service.
